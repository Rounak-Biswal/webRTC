1. The Public Internet (STUN/TURN Servers): Right now, your app only works if both tabs are on the same WiFi network (because it's only swapping local IP addresses). To make this work across the world, you need to pass an array of free Google "STUN" servers into your new RTCPeerConnection({ iceServers: [...] }) so the browsers can discover their public IP addresses.

2. Media Controls (Mute / Stop Video):
Adding buttons to toggle your microphone and camera on/off. (Hint: You don't drop the connection to do this; you just grab the specific audio or video track from your stream and set track.enabled = false).

3. Graceful Teardown:
Right now, if someone closes their tab during a call, the other person's video freezes. You'll want to add logic to catch the disconnect, close the RTCPeerConnection cleanly, and reset the UI so they can call someone else.

4. Call State UI:
Adding logic to handle "User is busy" (if they are already in a call) or creating a ringing UI while waiting for the other person to hit accept.
